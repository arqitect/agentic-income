# Executive Summary

## One-Page Summary

Eric Fadeyev can create income in Santa Cruz County fastest by selling simple lead-capture and follow-up systems to small local service businesses. The strongest starting point is not a broad "AI agency." It is a focused local operator model: find businesses that already get some demand, show where leads are being lost, fix the intake and follow-up flow, and charge a practical setup fee plus optional monthly support.

The first niche should be owner-operated exterior cleaning and adjacent property-maintenance businesses: pressure washing, window cleaning, gutter cleaning, solar panel cleaning, handyman/property maintenance, and small landscaping operators. These businesses usually have straightforward offers, visible website and intake issues, and shorter sales cycles than restaurants, hotels, or remodel firms.

## Recommended Business Model

Local Lead + Follow-Up System for home and property service businesses.

What Eric sells:

- cleaner quote/request forms
- landing pages or service pages
- lead tracking in Sheets/Airtable/Baserow
- follow-up reminders and message drafts
- review request systems
- short audit reports and local SEO page ideas

What Eric does not sell first:

- fully autonomous agents
- complex custom software
- ad management promises
- guaranteed rankings or guaranteed revenue
- giant CRM migrations

## Best First Niche

Exterior cleaning/property-maintenance businesses in Santa Cruz County.

Why:

- easy to find leads
- clear pain around missed calls and weak quote flows
- recurring local demand tied to coastal property upkeep
- simple before/after value story
- easier first sale than hospitality or contractor operations

## First Offer

First offer: `Quote Flow Fix`

Keep it tight enough to deliver in one focused week and roughly `4 to 6` hours of real work.

Includes:

- short website/contact-flow audit
- improved quote form or contact path
- simple lead tracker
- one manual follow-up reminder step
- review request template pack

## Pricing

- first paid beta: `$300 to $500`
- normal starter setup after proof: `$700 to $1,300`
- monthly support: `$99 to $249`

The first goal is not maximum price. It is a fast paid proof point, a repeatable delivery process, and one real client result.

## Why This Can Work In Santa Cruz

- Tourism and lodging activity keep service demand circulating across the county.
- Coastal homes, rentals, and property wear create practical maintenance demand.
- Santa Cruz County has a mix of city and unincorporated markets with many small operators.
- Many local businesses still run on phone calls, texts, and outdated web forms.
- Eric can use technical leverage privately while delivering plain-English outcomes.

## Biggest Risks

- overbuilding before a sale
- chasing too many niches at once
- offering custom work without boundaries
- relying on unverified market claims
- sending outreach in a spammy way
- underpricing complex work
- accidentally drifting into contractor brokering or other higher-liability activity
