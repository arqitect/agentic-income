# Sales Outreach

## Positioning

Use this frame:

"I am a local student/operator helping small service businesses tighten quote flow, follow-up, and review capture."

That lands better than "I run AI automations."

## Outreach Rules

- keep it short
- mention one specific observation
- ask permission before sending a longer audit
- do not oversell
- do not fake familiarity
- do not claim guaranteed outcomes
- do not cold text prospects
- if using cold email, include real sender identity, valid reply info, and a postal address
- do not use tourism or housing statistics as generic outreach hooks unless directly relevant to that business

## Daily Limits

- 5 to 10 cold emails
- 3 to 5 calls
- 1 to 3 walk-ins if physically nearby and appropriate
- 3 to 5 follow-ups

This keeps quality high and lowers spam risk.

## Week 1 Channel Priority

1. email
2. phone
3. in-person if local and natural

Leave Instagram DM as a backup, not the main channel.

## When To Stop Following Up

Stop when:

- the lead says no
- the lead asks not to be contacted
- 3 outreach attempts across 10 to 14 days get no response
- the business is clearly bad fit

## Ask-Permission Line

Use a line like:

"If helpful, I can send a short audit with the 3 things I'd fix first."

## Email Script Structure

1. short personal opener
2. one real observation
3. one practical outcome
4. permission-based CTA

## Follow-Up Sequence

### Touch 1

- short intro and permission ask

### Touch 2: 3 to 4 days later

- quick bump
- mention one specific observation

### Touch 3: 7 to 10 days later

- polite closeout
- "happy to send the short audit if useful; if not, no worries"

## Objection Handling

### "We're too busy."

"That usually means the best opportunity is making intake and follow-up lighter, not adding more work. I can keep the audit short."

### "We already have a website."

"Totally. I am not assuming you need a rebuild. Usually I am looking for small conversion and follow-up fixes."

### "We don't need AI."

"Understood. I am not really selling AI. I help clean up quote flow, review requests, and lead tracking."

### "How much does this cost?"

"For small setups, usually a few hundred to low four figures depending on scope. I'd rather look at the actual fit first than give you a padded number."

## Anti-Spam Rules

- do not mass blast
- personalize every first touch
- include real identity and contact info
- honor opt-outs immediately
- keep outreach volume modest
- do not scrape aggressively
