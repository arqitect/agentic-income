- one service page or landing page update

- one follow-up reminder process

### Timeline

- 7 to 10 business days after assets and payment are received

### Price

- beta: `$600 to $900`
- normal: `$900 to $1,300`

### Deliverables

- revised copy or page structure
- quote form fields
- lead tracker
- basic handoff instructions

- provide service areas
- provide phone/email/contact preference
- review drafts in reasonable time

### Limitations

- no full website redesign
- no ad management
- no guarantee of rankings or revenue

### Optional Monthly Support

- `$149 to $249`

### Acceptance Criteria

- form works on desktop and mobile
- lead appears in tracker
- follow-up process is documented
