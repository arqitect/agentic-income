## 1. Business Overview

What the business appears to do, who it likely serves, and what a new visitor probably wants most.

## 2. What They Do Well

- clear strengths
- strong trust signals
- good service coverage
- good photography or reviews

## 3. Missed Conversion Opportunities

- unclear or weak calls to action
- too many steps to request a quote
- no service-area clarity
- no fast path for mobile users

## 4. Quote / Contact Flow Issues

- is there a quote form?
- are fields useful or too vague?
- is phone the only option?
- is there a clear expectation of response time?
- is the contact page mobile-friendly?

## 5. Mobile Issues

- button visibility
- phone tap behavior
- form usability
- page speed or clutter observations

## 6. Review / Reputation Opportunities

- review count and freshness if visible
- missing review prompts
- no visible social proof on key pages
- GBP or testimonial gaps

## 7. Local SEO Opportunities

- missing city/service pages
- weak title/headline structure
- unclear service area
- lack of location-specific language
