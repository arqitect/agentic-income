# Niche Ranking

Scoring lens:

- fast first payment
- easy lead discovery
- clear pain
- simple fulfillment
- realistic budget for a new operator
- recurring support potential

`100` means "best fit for Eric right now," not "best industry in general."

Important: these are starting assumptions, not proven truth. After `20` real conversations, replace these scores with actual data on replies, calls booked, proposals, closes, and delivery pain.

## Recommended Order

1. Window cleaning
2. Pressure washing / exterior cleaning
3. Handyman / property maintenance
4. Gutter cleaning
5. Property-management support

## Final Recommendation

Start with the `exterior cleaning + property-maintenance` cluster, not because it is glamorous, but because it gives the best mix of:

- easy lead discovery
- visible website/intake problems
- short sales cycle
- simple first fulfillment
- clear owner ROI

After one or two wins, expand into property managers and selected vacation-rental operations, where monthly support value is stronger.

Do not start by selling all five top niches at once. In week 1, pick one micro-cluster only: `window cleaning + pressure washing + gutter cleaning`.
